Welcome to the Data Science Colloquium of the ENS.

This colloquium is organised around the theme of data science with the goal of bringing together many diverse researchers from ENS, PSL, and the Montagne Saint-Genevieve quarter from in a broad range of topics such as mathematics, computer science, physics, chemistry, neuroscience, and beyond.

The seminar takes place, unless exceptionally noted, on the first Tuesday of each month at 12h00 at the Physics Department of ENS, 24 rue Lhomond, in room CONF IV (2nd floor).

The colloquium is followed by an open buffet around which participants can meet and discuss collaborations.

These seminars are made possible by the support of the CFM-ENS Chaire "Modèles et Sciences des Données"

You can check the la list of the following seminars and the list of past seminars.

The organizers:

  • Florent Krzakala (ENS and UPMC)
  • Stéphane Mallat (ENS)
  • Gabriel Peyré (CNRS and ENS)
